Denver, CO

The city was founded on November 22, 1858.

Denver located toward the center of Colorado, along the Rocky Mountains front range. It is considered an urban city.

As of 2024, Denver has a population of around 700,000 Denverites.

The median salary in Denver is $56,381. Compared to the rest of the state's median of $47,346
