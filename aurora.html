Aurora, CO

The city was original incorporated as the Town of Fletcher on May, 5, 1903. It was later made the City of Aurora, in 1929.

Aurora is located to the east of Denver. It is a suburb of Denver.

As of 2024, Aurora is the state's third most populus city in Colorado with a population of 386,261 Aurorans.

The median salary in Aurora is $38,047. Compared to the rest of the state's median of $47,346
