Parker, CO

The town of Parker was incoporated into Colorado on May 1, 1981.

Parker is located south east of Denver. Parker is known as a rural town.

As of July 1, 2022, the town of Parker has 61,222 residents.

The median salary in Parker is around $52,000. Compared to the rest of the state's median of $47,346.
